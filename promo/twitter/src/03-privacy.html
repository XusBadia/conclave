ConclaveMD
Your patients' data never leaves your machine.
Not a policy. Normative invariants, enforced in the architecture — there is no Conclave server to send anything to.
01The core, rag & deident crates make no network calls.
02Patient text is de-identified before any prompt; the masked form is persisted, never the raw.
03Secrets live in the OS keychain; OAuth token files are written 0600.
04No telemetry. The webview runs under a strict Content-Security-Policy.
